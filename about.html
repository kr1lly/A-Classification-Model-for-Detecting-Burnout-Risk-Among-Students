About the Burnout Risk Detection System

This system is designed to help individuals assess their risk of burnout by evaluating various factors related to their work and personal life.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a simple and effective tool for individuals to understand their burnout risk and take proactive steps to improve their well-being.

How It Works

The system works by asking you a series of questions about your work environment, daily routines, and personal life. Based on your responses, it evaluates your risk of burnout and provides personalized recommendations.
